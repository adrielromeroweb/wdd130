Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to offer good and efficient information about the most famous places to rafting. This information could be locations, weather, rental price for tools, etc. Also this web site recomends the White Water Rafting company to organize all of this factor for the user.

Audience

The audience of this website is focused on people who are on vacation and looking to spend an exciting day by this recreational activity. These people are usually, first, a group of young people between the ages of 18 and 30, or, second, an entire family. In addition to this, all these people seek adrenaline and experience something they have never felt before.
